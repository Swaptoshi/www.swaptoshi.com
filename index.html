Product Roadmap

Discover our journey towards interoperable DEX protocol!

Devnet

Jan 24

During this phase, the first Swaptoshi DEX Preview will be deployed and prepared for testing by the community. This stage serves as an opportunity to gather initial input from the community.

Testnet

TBA

During this phase, the Testnet version will be deployed, and Swaptoshi will be linked to the Klayr mainchain testnet. This step provides an additional testing environment to ensure the smooth operation and security of Swaptoshi.

Mainnet

TBA

In this stage, the Mainnet version will be deployed, and Swaptoshi will be linked to the mainnet of the Klayr main chain, marking the official launch of Swaptoshi v1. Tokens on the Klayr mainchain, as well as other chains employing the same protocol, will be available for swapping through Swaptoshi DEX.

SDK

TBA

In this stage, Swaptoshi SDK will be released to npm. Four on-chain modules will be available under the SDK: DexModule, TokenFactoryModule, LiquidPosModule, and FeeConversionModule. Sidechain developer on Klayr can utilize our SDK to build their own unique use cases.

Governance

TBA

In this stage, the Governance module (LIP-0074) will be deployed to Swaptoshi Mainnet, marking the release of Swaptoshi v2. Governance module will enables Treasury management and Chain Configuration to be governed through decentralized proposals that can be voted on by the SWX holders.

Bridge

TBA

This marks the ultimate phase of Swaptoshi, where Swaptoshi Chain on Klayr integrates with Swaptoshi dApps on the Lisk network. This bridge will possible through upcoming Klayr Cross-Chain Bridge Module. This phase signifies the launch of Swaptoshi v3, embodying the realization of Swaptoshi's vision.
